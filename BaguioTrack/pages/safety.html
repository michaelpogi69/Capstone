Safety Tips & Guidelines

Stay safe while exploring Baguio City.

← Back to Home

General Safety & Personal Security

Be Aware of Surroundings: Pickpocketing can occur in busy areas like Session Road and Burnham Park.

Well-Lit Areas: Avoid walking alone in dark or isolated streets.

Trusted Accommodations: Book only accredited hotels or transient houses.

Avoid Scams: Watch out for overpriced tours and suspicious offers.

Compliance with Local Ordinances

Follow Traffic Rules: Use crosswalks and respect pedestrian signals.

No Smoking: Smoking or vaping is prohibited in public areas unless designated.

Proper Waste Disposal: Segregate biodegradable and non-biodegradable trash.

No Obstruction: Do not block sidewalks or public pathways.

Respect Nature: Keep parks and heritage areas clean.
