About Us

Baguio Track is your ultimate digital guide to exploring Baguio City. We provide tourists and locals with information on attractions, jeepney routes, restaurants, and cultural sites to make exploring the City of Pines easy and fun.

Our Mission

To provide an easy-to-use, reliable, and up-to-date platform that helps visitors explore Baguio efficiently while promoting local culture and tourism.

Our Vision

To become the go-to digital guide for Baguio City, empowering tourists and locals to discover hidden gems and navigate the city seamlessly.
